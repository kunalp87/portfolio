roject Name: Tic-Tac-Toe


roject link: https://kunalp87.github.io/TicTacToe/


Tic Tac Toe is a simple two-player game where the objective is to get three of your symbols in a row on a 3x3 grid. One player uses "X," and the other uses "O." Players take turns marking an empty square in the grid, aiming to place three of their symbols in a horizontal, vertical, or diagonal line. If a player succeeds in forming a line, they win. If all squares are filled without either player completing a line, the game ends in a draw. Tic Tac Toe is a classic, easy-to-learn game that encourages strategic thinking.

roject Name: Rock Paper Scissor


roject link: https://kunalp87.github.io/RPS/


Rock Paper Scissors is a simple hand game typically played between two people. Each player simultaneously forms one of three shapes with their hand: rock (a fist), paper (an open hand), or scissors (a fist with the index and middle fingers extended, forming a V). The outcome is determined by the rules:
Rock crushes scissors (rock wins).
Scissors cuts paper (scissors wins).
Paper covers rock (paper wins).
If both players choose the same shape, the game is a tie and can be replayed. Rock Paper Scissors is often used as a quick, fun decision-making game or as a tiebreaker.

roject Name: Pig Game


roject link: https://kunalp87.github.io/pigGame/


The Pig Game is a two-player dice game where the goal is to reach 100 points before your opponent. Each player takes turns rolling a dice, with three options on their turn:
Roll Dice: The player rolls a dice and adds the result to their current turn’s score. However, if they roll a 1, they lose all points for that turn, and it becomes the other player’s turn.
Hold: The player can choose to “hold,” adding their current turn’s score to their total score. After holding, the turn shifts to the other player.
New Game: This button resets the game, setting both players’ scores to zero to start a new game.
The first player to reach or exceed 100 points on their total score wins the game.
